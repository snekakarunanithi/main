Nethra Eye Care Centre

Excellence in Eye Care Across Kerala

Nethra Eye Care Centre, located in Irinjalakuda, Kerala, is a premier eye care institution committed to delivering world-class ophthalmic services. Established in 2003 by Dr. P.V. Thomas, and Dr Bastin V A, two visionary ophthalmologists, the center was founded with the mission to eradicate blindness and provide affordable, high-quality eye care to all. Over the years, Nethra Eye Care has evolved into a leading super-specialty eye hospital in South Kerala, recognized for its cutting-edge technology, advanced treatments, and patient-centered approach.

Vision

International eminence in ophthalmology

Mission

To lead the overall development of Ophthalmology by

  • Providing the highest quality, cost-effective ophthalmic care to the general public and to the economically weaker section in particular.
  • Treating everyone the way I want to be treated with kindness, respect, and dignity.

Core Values

  • Patient First – Ensuring the highest standards of safety, comfort, and care.
  • Integrity & Innovation – Upholding ethical practices while integrating state-of-the-art advancements in ophthalmology.
  • Accountability – Taking full responsibility for our services and patient outcomes.
  • Commitment to Excellence – Continuously upgrading our skills, facilities, and knowledge to provide the best eye care solutions.
